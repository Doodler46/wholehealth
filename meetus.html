Meet us!

Dr. Beth Catlin (Kolling), D.C.,B.S. Owner/Chiropractor

Dr. Beth Catlin was born and raised in Albert Lea, MN and now currently resides in Cottage Grove. She enjoys spending her free time with her husband and daughter, their family and friends, along with attending sporting events and being active. She loves being outdoors and gardening.

She loves working with patients of all ages helping with a variety of issues including neck and back pain, sciatica, extremity joint pain, headaches, vertebral disc issues, and others that are looking to improve their health.

Education

Dr. Beth attended University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire where she received a B.S. in Kinesiology with an emphasis in Exercise Science. She attended Northwestern Health Sciences University for her Chiropractic doctorate. She has also received her D.O.T. Medical Examiner Certification.

Being a long time student, Dr. Beth has a passion for learning and sharing her knowledge with the public. She also enjoys lecturing and working with community groups to improve health and wellness.

Dr. Beth is a member of the Minnesota Chiropractic Association (MCA) and the Cottage Grove Lions Club.

Chloe Rode Nutrition and Wellness Coach

Chloe is currently in school to become a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner through the Nutritional Therapy Association.

She has been drawn to studying health and wellness her whole life. She is committed to sharing her knowledge with others. Chloe's mission is to help people develop a healthy lifestyle that works for them through nutrition, wellness practices, and weight loss.

Chloe enjoys spending her free time with family and friends outdoors, hiking, exploring the twin cities, reading, and traveling.
